Understanding the Cycle

The Power-Possession Cycle describes a common emotional pattern where we seek external validation and power to fill an internal void. This creates a temporary high followed by an inevitable crash.

Breaking the cycle requires recognizing these patterns and developing internal sources of validation and emotional resilience.

💡 Key Insight:

The goal isn't to eliminate desire, but to understand its source and find healthier ways to meet your emotional needs.
