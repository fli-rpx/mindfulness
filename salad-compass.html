Salad Plate Compass

Your personal emotional navigation tool. When you're feeling off-balance, this compass helps you quickly identify your state and find the right action to restore equilibrium.

🥗 Salad Plate Compass

Your personal emotional navigation tool

How to Use This

  1. Print it, save it, or keep it on your phone. Make it immediately accessible.
  2. When you feel off-balance, quickly scan the "How It Feels" column and find the closest match.
  3. Ask yourself the Quick Self-Check question to confirm.
  4. Look at the One Action and do it—it takes less than 5 minutes.
  5. After the action, notice: Is the plate more balanced now?
Your State How It Feels Quick Self-Check The Missing Vegetable One Action to Balance
Power Excited, happy, optimistic "Am I riding high?" Connection Share your excitement with someone you trust—without needing anything back.
Possession Satisfied, full, occupied "Am I holding onto something too tightly?" Release Let go of one small thing—a plan, an opinion, a need to control. Notice how it feels.
Loss Disappointed, frustrated, powerless "Did something slip away?" Grieve Sit for 3 minutes and just name what you lost. No fixing. Just naming.
Emptiness Unrestful, slightly panicked, mindless "Is there a hollow feeling?" Ground 5-4-3-2-1 grounding: 5 things you see, 4 you touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.
Crave Like eating something—urgent, hungry "What am I hungry for right now?" Pause Wait 10 minutes before acting. Notice the urge in your body. Don't feed it—just watch it.

Why This Works

  • Fast Recognition: Colors and simple questions bypass overthinking
  • Immediate Action: Each state has a specific, doable response
  • Builds Awareness: Regular use trains your emotional intelligence
  • Portable Tool: Works anywhere, anytime you need grounding
